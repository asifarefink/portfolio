Leadership & Engagement

Executive Member - RUET Career Forum (RCF)

As an executive member of RUET Career Forum, I work towards bridging the gap between students and industry professionals, organizing career-related events, workshops, and career fairs to help students take their first step toward a successful career.

Assistant Organizing Secretary - Remians Community of RUET

As the Assistant Organizing Secretary, I play a crucial role in organizing various cultural, academic, and community-building events for the students of RUET. I am responsible for overseeing logistics, coordination, and ensuring smooth execution.

Founder - Synapse Retake (Esports Organization)

I am the proud founder of Synapse Retake, an esports organization aimed at fostering competitive gaming. We organize tournaments, training, and community events for passionate gamers, encouraging teamwork and skill development in the esports world.

Vocalist - Pandora's Box Band

As the lead vocalist of Pandora's Box, a popular music band in RUET, I contribute to creating a fusion of different music genres. We perform at events, providing a unique musical experience and fostering a creative atmosphere in the community.
