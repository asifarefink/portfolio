My Educational Institutions

Monipur High School, Mirpur

Location: Mirpur, Dhaka, Bangladesh

I completed my primary education at Monipur High School, where I took the Primary Education Completion (PEC) exam and achieved a GPA of 5, reflecting my dedication and hard work during those formative years.

Government Laboratory High School, New Market

Location: New Market, Dhaka, Bangladesh

From Class 6 to 8, I attended Government Laboratory High School. I appeared for the Junior School Certificate (JSC) exam and secured a GPA of 5, demonstrating consistent academic excellence during my early teenage years.

Dhaka Residential Model College (DRMC)

Location: Mohammadpur, Dhaka, Bangladesh

I pursued my secondary and higher secondary education at DRMC. I appeared for both the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) exams, achieving a GPA of 5 in each. Notably, in the HSC examination of 2019, DRMC achieved a 100% pass rate with 929 students obtaining GPA 5, underscoring the institution's commitment to academic excellence.

Established on January 1, 1960, DRMC operates under the Dhaka Education Board and is renowned for its rigorous academic standards and outstanding results.
