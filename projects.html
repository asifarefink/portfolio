GSM and GPS-based Vehicle Accident Detection & Driver Awareness System

The GSM and GPS-based Vehicle Accident Detection and Driver Awareness System is designed to enhance road safety by utilizing cutting-edge technologies. The system automatically detects accidents through sensors and immediately sends an alert with the vehicle's GPS coordinates to predefined emergency contacts, ensuring quick assistance. Additionally, it monitors driver behavior, detecting signs of fatigue or erratic driving, and provides timely alerts to the driver. This system is aimed at reducing accidents, promoting safe driving, and improving emergency response times.
